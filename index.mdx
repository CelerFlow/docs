## Say it once. It's done.

Tell CelerFlow what you need in plain English. The Planner reads your description, drafts a step-by-step workflow, and waits for your confirmation. Once you approve, CelerFlow runs every step automatically — no code, no config, no drag-and-drop.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in and create a workspace">
    Go to [celerflow.vercel.app](https://celerflow.vercel.app), sign in, and complete the onboarding flow to set up your workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect a tool">
    Head to Connections and link your first tool. Gmail is the fastest place to start.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe what you need">
    Open a new workflow and type what you want to happen in plain English. Be specific about the outcome — CelerFlow handles the rest.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm and deploy">
    Review the draft workflow the Planner generates. Confirm it and watch each step run in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
