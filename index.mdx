## What is Celerflow?

Celerflow gives teams **visibility and control** over their AI agents in production. Whether you're running agents built with OpenClaw, CrewAI, LangGraph, or any MCP-compatible framework, Celerflow provides a unified control plane for monitoring health, enforcing policies, and auditing tool usage.

Think of it as the **ops layer** your AI agents are missing.

  <Card title="Fleet management" icon="server">

  </Card>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a fleet">
    Sign in to Celerflow and create a fleet to group your agents by project, team, or environment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your agents">
    Use the `ocauthz` CLI to authenticate and connect agents to your fleet. Each agent gets a bootstrap token for secure communication.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set policies">
    Configure tool-level permissions: allow, block, or require human-in-the-loop confirmation for specific tools.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor and audit">
    Watch agent health in real time. Review traces, analyze tool usage patterns, and export audit logs when needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
