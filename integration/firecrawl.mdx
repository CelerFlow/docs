## What this connection enables

Firecrawl is the core search provider for the **Web Search Agent** (`composio/firecrawl`) and the fetch backend for the **Scraper Agent** (`composio-firecrawl` runtime adapter). Use it to scout the web for relevant URLs and fetch full page content — it is not a general-purpose API surface.

## Connect it

From **Settings → Connections**, click **Connect Firecrawl**. A popup runs OAuth and the card flips to **Connected** once Composio confirms. See [Connections](/concepts/connections) for scope, stale-account handling, and polling behavior.

## Scopes and permissions

Firecrawl is an API-key-based Composio integration with no user-facing OAuth scopes. Composio manages the key on your behalf after you connect.

## Known limitations

Scout and fetch are the only supported operations in v0. Rate limits are enforced by Composio and depend on your plan.
