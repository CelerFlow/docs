## What this connection enables

The **Archiver Agent** uses Gmail as a delivery destination. Workflows can send a result — a summary, analysis, or file link — directly to an email recipient without leaving the platform.

## Connect it

From **Settings → Connections**, click **Connect Gmail**. A popup runs OAuth and the card flips to **Connected** once Composio confirms. See [Connections](/concepts/connections) for scope, stale-account handling, and polling behavior.

## Scopes and permissions

Gmail access is scoped to sending on behalf of the authenticated user. Read access to your inbox is not requested.
