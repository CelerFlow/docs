## What this connection enables

Workflows can read from and write to a HubSpot portal using the actions Composio exposes — pulling contact records, updating deal stages, or pushing enriched data back into the CRM.

## Connect it

From **Settings → Connections**, click **Connect HubSpot**. A popup runs OAuth and the card flips to **Connected** once Composio confirms. See [Connections](/concepts/connections) for scope, stale-account handling, and polling behavior.

## Scopes and permissions

You select a HubSpot portal at connect time. The connection is scoped to that portal — workflows cannot reach other portals without a separate connection.

## Known limitations

The specific HubSpot objects and actions available through Composio may expand over time. Treat the current set as a starting point, not a fixed surface.
