## What this connection enables

The **Archiver Agent** uses Slack as a delivery destination. When a workflow completes, results can be posted to a Slack channel of your choice — useful for surfacing summaries or analysis without switching context.

## Connect it

From **Settings → Connections**, click **Connect Slack**. A popup runs OAuth and the card flips to **Connected** once Composio confirms. See [Connections](/concepts/connections) for scope, stale-account handling, and polling behavior.

## Scopes and permissions

Slack access is scoped to posting messages to channels the OAuth user can see. The integration does not read message history.

## Known limitations

**Beta.** Some Slack delivery flows still route through a `coming_soon` path, and legacy `slack_not_connected` copy appears in parts of the UI. See [Roadmap](/platform/roadmap) for where this is heading.
