---
title: "Audit log"
description: "Export a record of team activity for compliance and review."
---

CelerFlow keeps a server-side record of actions on your team — workflows built and deployed, connections added and removed, runs triggered, member changes. You can export this record as JSON or CSV on demand.

<Note>
  Audit log export is available on the **Pro** plan and above. See [Billing](/platform/billing).
</Note>

## Exporting

From the audit settings surface, pick a date range and a format (**JSON** or **CSV**), then create the export. When it finishes, you'll see the row count and a download link. Past exports stay available for re-download.

## What gets recorded

Activity from every team member is recorded with the actor, action type, and a timestamp. Examples:

- Workflow created, deployed, redeployed, or deleted.
- Run triggered, reran, or cancelled.
- Connection added, refreshed, or removed.
- Member invited, role changed, or removed.
