## Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Select a plan and billing interval.
3. Complete payment via Stripe Checkout.
4. Your plan upgrades immediately — new limits take effect on next page load.

## Pro trial

Pro plan includes a **14-day free trial** (first subscription only, no credit card required for trial period).

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage subscription** in Settings → Billing to:

- Change your plan (upgrade/downgrade)
- Update payment method
- Cancel subscription
- View invoices

All subscription management goes through Stripe's Customer Portal.

## Downgrade behavior

When you downgrade or cancel:

- Access continues until the end of your billing period.
- Trace data beyond the new retention limit is cleaned up by the nightly cron.
- Agents beyond the new limit are **not** disconnected — you just can't add new ones.
- Team features (RBAC, audit export) become read-only.
