Every CelerFlow workspace lives under a plan. Plans determine how many workflow runs you can execute per billing period, how many workspaces you can own, and how far back your run history is kept.

## Plans

| Plan | Executions / month | Workspaces | Members | Run history |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | 20 | 1 | 1 | 3 days |
| **Pro** | 1,000 | 3 | 5 | 30 days |
| **Enterprise** | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | 365 days |

Limits are enforced server-side. Once you exhaust your executions for the period, new workflow runs are blocked until the period resets or you upgrade.

## What counts as an execution

One row in the `workflow_runs` table = one execution. Every time a workflow runs — including reruns — it counts once. Drafts, failed deploys that never reach Trigger.dev, and planner-side conversations do not count.

## Billing period

If you have an active subscription, the period tracks your subscription's `current_period_start` / `current_period_end`. Otherwise the period falls back to the calendar month. You can see the current period and used/remaining executions in **Settings → Billing**.

## Upgrading and downgrading

Upgrade or manage your subscription from **Settings → Billing**. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current period.
