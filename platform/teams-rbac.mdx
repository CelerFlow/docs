## Overview

Teams let multiple users share access to fleets, agents, traces, and policies with role-based permissions.

## Roles

| Role | View dashboard | View traces | Terminate agent | Edit policies | Manage members | Billing |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Owner** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Admin** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| **Operator** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| **Viewer** | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

## Creating a team

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Enter a team name and click **Create team**.
3. You become the Owner automatically.

## Inviting members

1. In the Team page, enter the member's email and select a role.
2. Click **Invite**.
3. The invited user will see the team in their Settings → Team page.
4. They click **Accept** to join.

<Note>
  The invited user must have an existing CelerFlow account.
</Note>

## Sharing fleets with a team

Assign a fleet to a team by updating the fleet's `team_id`. Team members can then see all agents and traces in that fleet, subject to their role permissions.

## Switching teams

If you belong to multiple teams, use the team selector in Settings → Team to switch context. Your personal workspace is always accessible.

## Removing members

Owners and Admins can remove members from the Team page. Removing a member revokes their access immediately.

<Note>
  The Owner cannot be removed. To transfer ownership, contact support.
</Note>
