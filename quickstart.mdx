<Info>
  **Prerequisites**: a CelerFlow account at [celerflow.vercel.app](https://celerflow.vercel.app) and a Gmail account you're willing to OAuth. Nothing to install locally.
</Info>

## Build your first workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in and create a workspace">
    Go to [celerflow.vercel.app](https://celerflow.vercel.app), sign in, and complete onboarding. Pick a use-case and give the workspace a name. Your default platform agents are seeded automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Gmail">
    From **Settings → Connections**, click **Connect Gmail**. A popup runs Google OAuth. The card flips to **Connected** once the connection is confirmed — usually within a few seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open a new workflow">
    From the dashboard, click **New workflow**. Describe the outcome in one or two sentences. For example:

    > When a new inbound lead email arrives, summarize it and file it to my Leads Notion database.

    Be specific about the trigger, the action, and the destination.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Answer the Planner's follow-ups">
    The Planner may ask a clarifying question, surface a connection card if another tool is missing, or pause to let you pick a Notion target. Answer in chat or complete the connection. See [Approvals](/concepts/approvals) for what each pause means.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm and deploy">
    When the draft looks right, the Planner asks **"Ready to deploy?"**. Confirm it and the workflow goes live. Redeploying later upgrades the same workflow in place — no duplicates.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Watch it run">
    Open the workflow from the dashboard to see run history, step-level logs, and the result. Updates stream in real time. See [Executions](/dashboard/executions) for the full detail view.
  </Step>
</Steps>
